## Art Styles Encyclopedia

The style you select shapes every visual element — line weight, color saturation,
character proportion, and animation fluidity. Choose one primary style and optional
secondary accent.

### Classic Disney (1940s–1960s)
- Smooth, almost liquid character movement; expressive proportions; warm, naturalistic
  color palettes; soft anti-aliased line work (2–3px weight)
- Large, sparkling eyes with white pupils; soft shadows using color gradation rather
  than pure black; rounded, flowing anatomy
- **Prompt cue:** "Classic Disney 1950s animation, soft curves, expressive eyes with
  white pupils, warm gradient backgrounds, smooth curved lines"
- **Ideal for:** Fairy tales, emotional character moments, magical sequences

### Cartoon Network / Warner Bros. Rubber Hose
- Bold, thick black outlines (4–6px); exaggerated impossible anatomy; high-contrast
  color blocks; minimal gradient work
- Comedic "takes" (rapid extreme expressions); speed lines radiating from movement;
  impact frames
- **Prompt cue:** "Cartoon Network classic style, bold 5px black outlines, rubber hose
  limbs, comedic timing, exaggerated takes"
- **Ideal for:** Comedy, action sequences, slapstick, energetic character interactions

### Flat Vector / Modern Minimalist
- Solid color shapes with NO gradients or textures; geometric construction; uniform thin
  outlines or no outline; modern, app-design aesthetic
- Limited 3–5 color palette per scene; smooth mathematical motion
- **Prompt cue:** "Flat vector animation, geometric shapes, solid color blocks, no
  gradients, modern design aesthetic, clean circles and rectangles"
- **Ideal for:** Explainers, motion graphics, infographics, corporate video

### Retro Rubber Hose (1920s–1930s)
- Impossibly stretchy, sinuous limbs following no anatomical logic; character eyes:
  two simple circles with dot pupils; art deco-inspired backgrounds; hypnotic fluidity
- **Prompt cue:** "1920s rubber hose animation, sinuous stretchy limbs, art deco
  background, hypnotic spiraling motion, impossible limb deformation"
- **Ideal for:** Period pieces, vintage advertising, whimsical storytelling

### Pencil Sketch / Hand-Drawn
- Visible pencil stroke texture throughout; sketchy line work; shading via
  cross-hatching, not gradients; organic wobble in all lines
- **Prompt cue:** "Pencil sketch animation, visible pencil strokes, rough gestural
  line work, cross-hatching shading, hand-drawn imperfection"
- **Ideal for:** Personal narratives, storybook adaptations, character-driven drama

### Watercolor / Painterly
- Soft blended colors with translucent layers; diffusion and feathering at edges;
  wet-on-wet color mixing visible; flowing, organic shapes
- **Prompt cue:** "Watercolor animation, soft blended colors, translucent layering,
  wet-on-wet diffusion, painterly brushwork, dreamy quality"
- **Ideal for:** Fairy tales, emotional drama, dreamy sequences, artistic expression

### Paper Cutout / Papier-Mache
- Flat, layered paper sheets stacked at slight angles creating depth; visible crease
  lines and fold edges; shadow cast by offset layers creates 3D illusion
- **Prompt cue:** "Paper cutout animation, layered colored paper sheets, visible
  creases and folds, cast shadows defining depth, paper grain texture"
- **Ideal for:** Children's stories, tactile narratives, DIY/craft themes

### Pixel Art / 8-bit and 16-bit Retro
- Small square pixels forming all image content; chunky geometric silhouettes;
  limited color palette (8–32 colors typical); no anti-aliasing or blur
- **Prompt cue:** "Pixel art animation, 16-bit retro video game style, geometric
  blocky shapes, limited color palette, game-style tile backgrounds"
- **Ideal for:** Retro gaming content, indie game aesthetics, nostalgia marketing

### Neon Line Art / Glow
- Glowing luminous lines on dark background; electric colors; minimal fill; heavy
  bloom and glow effects around all strokes
- **Prompt cue:** "Neon line art animation, glowing electric lines, dark background,
  magenta and cyan, heavy bloom effect, cyberpunk neon style"
- **Ideal for:** Cyberpunk themes, music videos, sci-fi opening sequences

### Manga / Anime
- Large, highly expressive eyes with multiple eyelash lines; sharp, defined linework;
  selective solid-color shading; dramatic speed lines and motion effects
- **Prompt cue:** "Manga anime style, large expressive eyes, dramatic speed lines,
  sharp black outlines, intense expressions, halftone shading"
- **Ideal for:** Action sequences, dramatic emotional beats, youth-oriented content

### Stop-Motion / Claymation
- Subtle frame-to-frame jitter; visible clay/puppet material texture; realistic film
  lighting; character has actual three-dimensional form
- **Prompt cue:** "Claymation stop-motion aesthetic, subtle frame jitter, visible clay
  texture, realistic dimensional lighting, fingerprints visible"
- **Ideal for:** Whimsical narratives, children's content, character-driven shorts

### Silhouette / Shadow Puppet
- Character is pure solid black silhouette; all detail comes from pose and gesture;
  background is richly detailed; backlighting creates silhouette effect
- **Prompt cue:** "Silhouette animation, pure black character shapes, detailed colorful
  background, shadow puppet aesthetic, dramatic backlighting"
- **Ideal for:** Dramatic sequences, poetic narratives, mysterious themes

### CGI Stylized / 3D Cel-Shading
- 3D computer-generated models with cartoon shader; visible bold outline edges; solid
  color blocks with hard-edged shadow lines; smooth 3D motion with 2D visual result
- **Prompt cue:** "3D cel-shaded cartoon, toon shader bold outlines, solid color blocks
  with hard shadows, smooth 3D motion, cartoon appearance from 3D models"
- **Ideal for:** Modern gaming aesthetics, high-production cartoons, stylized action

## Animation Principles in Prompt Language

All 12 core animation principles translate directly to prompt language:

| Principle | Prompt Keywords | Application |
|-----------|----------------|-------------|
| **Squash & Stretch** | `squash and stretch`, `rubber physics`, `volume preservation` | "Include exaggerated squash on landing, stretch during jump; preserve character volume" |
| **Anticipation** | `wind-up`, `anticipatory pose`, `subtle setup` | "Character winds up before major movement; crouches before jump" |
| **Staging** | `clear staging`, `focal hierarchy`, `layered depth` | "Clear focal point; secondary action doesn't distract from main action" |
| **Follow-Through** | `follow-through motion`, `trailing limbs`, `overlapping arcs` | "Hair and clothing lag slightly behind head movement; fingers last to settle" |
| **Arc Motion** | `arcing trajectory`, `curved motion path`, `smooth parabolic arc` | "Avoid straight-line movement; character moves in smooth curved paths" |
| **Timing & Spacing** | `rapid timing`, `slow-in slow-out`, `eased arcs`, `snappy beats` | "Snappy = fewer frames; floaty = evenly-spaced frames" |
| **Secondary Action** | `secondary gestures`, `independent movement`, `supporting action` | "Head turns before body; fingers gesture while speaking" |
| **Exaggeration** | `exaggerated`, `over-the-top`, `amplified emotion` | "Push emotion beyond realistic; eyes wider, mouth larger, movements bigger than life" |
| **Solid Drawing** | `model consistency`, `accurate proportions`, `maintained character design` | "Proportions held throughout; perspective accurate" |
| **Appeal / Charm** | `appealing design`, `charming expressions`, `personality-driven` | "Character has personality in every pose; expressions are engaging" |
| **Emotional Authenticity** | `genuine emotion beneath exaggeration`, `sincere character motivation` | "Even in cartoony world, emotional motivation is clear and sincere" |
| **Loose Ends / Imperfection** | `slight line imperfection`, `hand-drawn quality`, `organic unevenness` | "Allow slight wonkiness in line work for hand-drawn feel" |

## Character Design Principles

### Proportions and Silhouette
- **Rule of Thirds:** Head, torso, legs should read clearly as distinct sections
- **Silhouette Test:** Fill character with solid black — should be readable and unique
- **Feature Exaggeration:** Enlarge distinctive features (large eyes, long ears, round belly)
- **Asymmetry:** One feature slightly off-balance makes character more memorable

### Facial Expressions
- **Eyes:** Largest single feature communicating emotion; use size changes, pupil placement, eyebrow angle
- **Mouth:** Extreme shapes for surprise, sadness, joy; overlaps eyes at intensity
- **Squash & Stretch on Face:** Face itself deforms to show emotion (puffed cheeks for anger, narrowed eyes for focus)
- **Micro-Expressions:** Include momentary extreme shapes (0.1–0.2 sec) between neutral frames

## Line Work Styles

| Style | Weight | Effect | Prompt |
|-------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Thick Bold Outline** | 4–8px, consistently heavy | Strong graphic quality, reads at distance | "Thick 5px black outlines, bold graphic silhouettes, high contrast" |
| **Thin Line** | 1–2px, uniform | Clean, refined, contemporary | "Thin uniform 1.5px black outlines, clean modern vector feel" |
| **Variable Weight** | 2–6px depending on direction | Artistic, hand-drawn authenticity | "Variable weight outlines, heavier on shadows, gestural hand-drawn quality" |
| **No Outline** | None | Modern, sophisticated, minimalist | "No outlines, color and value define form, flat vector aesthetic" |
| **Textured / Sketchy** | Multiple imperfect passes | Raw, energetic, hand-crafted | "Sketchy textured outlines, visible pencil strokes, rough artistic feel, hatching details" |

## Background Approaches

| Approach | Technique | Mood | Prompt |
|----------|-----------|------|--------|
| **Detailed Painted** | Fully rendered, illustrative background art | Rich, immersive | "Richly detailed painted background, soft impressionist garden, character integrated naturally" |
| **Simplified Graphic** | Flat shapes, minimal detail, color blocks | Modern, minimalist, focus-driven | "Simplified graphic background, flat color blocks, character as clear focal point" |
| **Abstract Gradient** | Soft color gradients, no recognizable forms | Timeless, universal, intimate | "Abstract gradient background, soft purple to pink fade, no recognizable forms" |
| **Pattern / Repeating** | Wallpaper-like geometric or organic repeat | Whimsical, rhythmic | "Repeating star and moon pattern background, pastel colors, rhythmic tile effect" |
| **Fully Dynamic** | Background actively animates, morphs | Surreal, magical | "Background actively morphs and shifts, clouds become shapes, ground waves like water" |

## Character Animation Keywords

### Walk Cycles

| Walk Type | Keywords | Timing |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| **Happy Walk** | `bouncy stride, weight shifts hip-to-hip, arms swing loosely, head bobs, light footfalls` | Quick rhythmic 8–12 frame cycles |
| **Angry March** | `stiff heavy footfalls, arms pump forcefully, jaw clenched, rigid spine` | Forceful, heavy 10–14 frame cycles |
| **Sneaky Tiptoe** | `raised heel walk, exaggerated caution, slow deliberate pace, eyes darting` | Slow, deliberate 16–20 frame cycles |
| **Confident Stride** | `long purposeful steps, head held high, shoulders back, smooth gait` | Moderate, controlled 12–16 frame cycles |
| **Floating / Weightless** | `minimal foot contact, slow gliding motion, upward-drifting quality, loose limbs` | Slow, smooth 20–28 frame cycles |
| **Exhausted Shuffle** | `dragging feet, slouched posture, minimal arm motion, head down` | Slow, heavy 18–24 frame cycles |

### Facial Expression Keywords

| Emotion | Features | Keywords |
|---------|----------|----------|
| **Happiness / Joy** | Wide-open upturned eyes, broad smile, rosy cheeks | `bright smile, sparkling eyes, rosy cheeks, joyful grin, eye crinkles` |
| **Sadness / Sorrow** | Drooping eyelids, downturned pupils, quivering lip | `drooping eyes, quivering lip, single tear roll, downturned mouth, slumped shoulders` |
| **Anger / Rage** | Narrow slits, bared teeth, eyebrows angled down | `narrowed eyes, bared teeth, flushed red, clenched jaw, smoke from nostrils` |
| **Fear / Terror** | Extremely wide eyes, white showing, open O-shape mouth | `wide shocked eyes, open mouth scream, trembling, rigid tension, hair standing` |
| **Surprise / Shock** | Wide open, pupils dilated, head jerk backward | `wide open mouth shock, bulging eyes, head jerk, impact frame` |
| **Exhaustion** | Heavily lidded, half-closed, dark circles | `heavily lidded eyes, drooping posture, sweat drops, dark circles, tongue out` |

## Transition and Effect Library

### Cartoon Wipes

| Effect | Keywords | Timing | Use |
|--------|----------|--------|-----|
| **Speed Line Burst** | `speed lines radiate outward, circular wipe from center, motion blur effect` | 0.3–0.5 s | Dynamic, energetic transition |
| **Circular Iris Wipe** | `circular opening or closing, iris-style transition, gradual reveal/conceal` | 0.3–0.7 s | Classic, theatrical |
| **Splat / Ink Bloom** | `colored splat radiates outward, organic splash shape, ink-like diffusion` | 0.2–0.5 s | Fun, playful, organic |
| **Diagonal Swipe** | `diagonal line sweeps across screen, sharp or soft edge, directional motion` | 0.2–0.4 s | Quick, snappy, dynamic |

### Impact and Action Effects

| Effect | Keywords | Duration | Use |
|--------|----------|----------|-----|
| **Impact Frame / Freeze** | `single frame holds on extreme pose, exaggerated expression, motion freezes momentarily` | 0.1–0.3 s | Emphasize impact, surprise |
| **Speed Lines / Motion Trails** | `curved or straight lines follow character motion, radiating from center, speed emphasis` | Dynamic | Emphasize velocity |
| **Smear Frame** | `character stretches into blurred streak following motion path, follow-through emphasized` | 1–3 frames | Fast direction changes |
| **Screen Shake / Jitter** | `camera vibrates on impact, rapid position shifts, intensity decreases over time` | 0.1–0.4 s | Impact moments, comedic bumps |

### Particle and Sparkle Effects

| Effect | Keywords | Duration | Use |
|--------|----------|----------|-----|
| **Sparkle Burst** | `golden or silver sparkles radiate outward, twinkling effect, magical quality` | 0.3–0.6 s | Magic, celebration, joy |
| **Confetti Cascade** | `colorful confetti pieces rain down or erupt upward, rotating particles` | 0.5–1.5 s | Celebration, victory |
| **Smoke / Cloud Puff** | `billowing smoke cloud, expanding and dissipating, organic wispy motion` | 0.4–1.0 s | Disappearance, comedic exit |
| **Heart Float** | `pink/red hearts drift upward or float in circle, romantic or lovestruck effect` | 0.5–1.5 s | Love reaction, happiness |
