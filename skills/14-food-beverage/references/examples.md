## Master Template

```

SCENE SETUP
[Specific location, time of day, ambiance]

HERO ELEMENT
[The dish, drink, or ingredient that is the star]

OPENING HOOK (0–2 seconds)
[One of the 2-second hooks from hooks.md. Build hunger immediately.]

PRIMARY MOVEMENTS
[How the food is prepared, plated, or consumed. What actions unfold?]

CAMERA WORK
[Specific camera movements: slow push, orbiting, overhead, close-up, etc.]

LIGHTING APPROACH
[Key light direction, backlight for steam, side light for texture, colour temperature]

SOUND DESIGN
[Which ASMR elements: sizzle, crunch, pour, fizz? Layer them strategically.]

[Colours, props, surface, garnish, steam timing, condensation, etc.]
```

## Example 1: Fine Dining — Pan-Seared Scallop

```
SCENE SETUP
Upscale restaurant kitchen or plating station at golden hour. Soft warm light from
overhead window. Minimalist white plate in foreground. Chef's hands visible —
professional and precise.

HERO ELEMENT
Three perfectly seared scallops with golden crust, arranged in geometric triangle on
white plate. Microgreens, edible flowers, sea salt crystals visible. Beurre blanc
sauce in a delicate line across plate.

OPENING HOOK (0–2 seconds)
Camera starts on spoon of beurre blanc held overhead. Sauce being drizzled in
slow-motion onto the white plate in a deliberate, artistic line. Backlit to show the
glossy, silky sauce catch light. Steam gently rising from the warm sauce.
This is the appetite trigger — luxury in liquid form.

PRIMARY MOVEMENTS
1. Chef's hand placing first scallop (top of triangle)
2. Microgreens placed with tweezers — extreme precision
3. Edible flower placed (single deliberate action)
4. Beurre blanc final drizzle
5. Chef's hand framing the dish

CAMERA WORK
Start overhead (scallop placement); slow push-in as garnish added; switch to low
45° angle as beurre blanc poured (backlit); final tight macro on sauce and scallop.

LIGHTING APPROACH
Warm key light 45° stage-left. Backlight for beurre blanc gloss. Side light on
scallops showing golden sear crust. Minimal shadows; pristine white plate stays white.

SOUND DESIGN
Soft sizzle of recent sear (low in mix). Tweezers placing microgreens (quiet ping
sounds). Gentle pour of beurre blanc. Subtle ambience: kitchen hum, faint music.

FOOD STYLING NOTES
Scallop sear marks visible but not overdone. Beurre blanc glossy but not pooling
chaotically. Microgreens fresh and vibrant. Plate edges pristine white.
Sea salt crystals catching light.

CLOSEUP MOMENTS
Macro on sauce drizzle and gloss. Macro on golden sear crust. Macro on microgreen
detail. Macro on edible flower.

THE MONEY SHOT
Beurre blanc drizzle completing, sauce pooling next to scallop, gloss catching
backlight, steam ghosting across the plate.

COLOUR PALETTE
White plate negative space; golden-brown scallops; pale golden sauce; vibrant
microgreen green; accent of edible flower colour.

MOOD AND PACING
Slow, luxurious, elegant, sophisticated. Each element gets time to be appreciated.
Warmth of lighting signals "this is special."

PARAMETERS
Model: seedance_2_0
Duration: 8–12 s
Aspect ratio: 9:16 or 1:1
Resolution: 1080p
Genre: drama
```

## Example 2: Café — Latte Art Pour

```
SCENE SETUP
Warm, busy modern café. Morning light streaming through large windows. Barista in
soft focus behind counter. Espresso cup on saucer on bar counter. Milk pitcher
steaming in barista's hand.

HERO ELEMENT
The final 3 seconds of latte art being poured. Completed design (leaf, heart, or
rosetta) coming into full view as the pour concludes.

OPENING HOOK (0–2 seconds)
Camera tight on espresso cup. Deep brown espresso with thin crema visible. Steam
rising. Pan upward to reveal milk pitcher entering frame from top. Builds
anticipation — the pour is about to happen.

PRIMARY MOVEMENTS
1. Milk pitcher begins pour (pour stream visible)
2. White microfoam mixing with espresso (colour change visible)
3. Barista's wrist control visible, arm creating design
4. Final pour separation as design completes
5. Pitcher pulling away
6. Finished latte with completed art revealed

CAMERA WORK
Start tight on cup at 45° angle (showing espresso and crema). Track as pitcher
enters. Stay tight as pour begins (see milk stream). Subtle push-back as design
completes. Slight zoom on finished art as final reveal.

LIGHTING APPROACH
Warm diffused window light (naturally soft). Side light catching milk steam and cup
rim. Warm colour temp (3000 K–3200 K) reinforces "cosy morning."

SOUND DESIGN
Quiet espresso machine buzz (far back). Soft pour of milk into espresso (main
sound). The quiet clink of pitcher removing from cup. Ambient café sounds (quiet
chatter, subtle music).

FOOD STYLING NOTES
Espresso crema freshly visible. Milk steam rising (shot as steaming ends). Cup warm
and "just made." Ceramic white or cream (contrast with brown espresso).
Latte art clean and well-executed. Saucer visible with small spoon.

CLOSEUP MOMENTS
Macro on espresso crema detail. Macro on milk steam. Macro on final latte art
design in sharp focus. Barista's wrist angle.

THE MONEY SHOT
The exact moment the latte art design is fully revealed as the pitcher pulls away.
Smooth glossy foam art in perfect definition. Backlit milk steam around the design.

COLOUR PALETTE
Warm browns, cream whites, golden milk foam, warm ceramic, soft morning light.

MOOD AND PACING
Cosy, skilled, inviting, ritualistic. Slow and meditative. This is about craft.

PARAMETERS
Model: seedance_2_0
Duration: 8–12 s
Aspect ratio: 9:16 or 1:1
Resolution: 1080p
Genre: drama
```

## Example 3: Fast Food — Premium Burger Cheese Pull

```
SCENE SETUP
Bright energetic food photography studio. Burger on wax paper or neutral surface.
Slight depth blur in background. Warm, saturated lighting.

HERO ELEMENT
Gourmet burger with visible layers: seeded bun, fresh lettuce, juicy patty, melted
American cheese hanging from patty, tomato, onion, special sauce.

OPENING HOOK (0–2 seconds)
Camera at burger side angle. Top bun being slowly lifted away. As bun separates,
melted cheese stretches downward, reaching maximum elasticity — strand still
connecting patty to bun. Backlight glints off the cheese.
Sizzle sound. Viewer thinks: "WAIT I WANT THAT."

PRIMARY MOVEMENTS
1. Hand lifting top bun (slow, deliberate)
2. Cheese stretch revealing (peak moment — maximum length before breaking)
3. Strand breaking (the tear)
4. Cheese draping back onto patty
5. Full burger reveal showing all layers

CAMERA WORK
Start at burger side, bun at top of frame. Smooth push-in as bun lifts. Camera stays
locked on the cheese stretch moment. Final frame shows full burger composition.

LIGHTING APPROACH
Warm key light 45° showing burger depth. Backlight on the cheese strand specifically
(makes the stretch glow). Slight bottom bounce light showing patty sear crust.

SOUND DESIGN
Sizzle (fresh patty — must be present). Subtle tear sound of cheese breaking.
Background: energetic modern music or café ambiance.

FOOD STYLING NOTES
Melted cheese absolutely visible and glossy. Patty still juicy and seared (char marks
visible). Lettuce vibrant green. Tomato ripe and juicy-looking. Bun golden and
seeded. Sauce glossy. Everything slightly glistening.

CLOSEUP MOMENTS
Macro on cheese stretch showing elasticity. Macro on patty sear crust. Macro on
lettuce texture. Macro on sauce shine.

THE MONEY SHOT
Cheese at absolute maximum stretch, still connecting bun to patty, backlit so the
cheese glows. The exact frame where the stretch reaches its limit before breaking.

COLOUR PALETTE
Golden bun, vibrant green lettuce, rich red tomato, golden cheese, dark-seared
patty on warm neutral background.

MOOD AND PACING
Energetic, indulgent, bold. Bright colours. Hunger-triggering. Quick cuts between
angles around hero moment (hero moment in slow-motion).

PARAMETERS
Model: seedance_2_0
Duration: 8–12 s
Aspect ratio: 9:16
Resolution: 1080p
Genre: action
```

## Example 4: Cocktail Bar — Spirit Pour and Ice

```
SCENE SETUP
Upscale cocktail bar at night. Moody sophisticated lighting. Crystal glassware.
Bar top slightly wet with condensation. Barista/mixologist in soft focus.
Premium spirits visible in backlit bottles.

HERO ELEMENT
Classic Old Fashioned or Manhattan. Crystal rocks glass. 2–3 large ice cubes.
Bourbon or rye being poured over ice. Amber / deep liquid colour.

OPENING HOOK (0–2 seconds)
Bottle of spirit positioned above rocks glass at 45°. Camera tight on the interaction
point where spirit meets ice. Pour begins: amber liquid cascades over ice, ice cubes
shift and clink, liquid spreads. The colour of the spirit is rich and appetising.

PRIMARY MOVEMENTS
1. Bottle tilting to pour position
2. Spirit flowing into glass (capture the stream)
3. Ice cubes shifting with impact
4. Liquid pooling and spreading around ice
5. Bitters being added (dark droplets creating cloud effect in liquid)
6. Bar spoon stirring (craft signal)
7. Citrus peel twist being expressed (orange oils spraying subtly)
8. Final positioned glass on bar

CAMERA WORK
Tight on pour from 45° side angle (show depth). Stay locked as spirit fills.
Possible subtle push-in as final garnish placed. Final wide shot showing finished
drink on bar in context.

LIGHTING APPROACH
Warm moody bar lighting (2700 K, slightly dimmed). Backlight on bottles (depth,
shows sophistication). Side light on glass and spirit (showing colour and
transparency). Light on ice showing crystalline detail.

SOUND DESIGN
Glug of spirit pouring (not rushed, slightly thick sound). Ice cubes clinking as
liquid hits them. Ice tinkling as bar spoon stirs. Citrus peel snapping/twisting.
Ambient bar sounds: faint conversation, subtle jazz, gentle clinking.

FOOD STYLING NOTES
Spirit colour rich and visible (not so dark it is invisible). Ice cubes large and
sculptural (not crushed). Glass clear crystal (pristine). Bar top with subtle wet
spots. Bar spoon elegant. Citrus peel bright orange or lemon yellow.

CLOSEUP MOMENTS
Macro on spirit pouring and ice cubes. Macro on ice crystalline structure.
Macro on bitters cloud spreading in liquid. Macro on citrus peel oils being expressed.

THE MONEY SHOT
Spirit stream hitting ice, ice cubes glinting, liquid cascading and spreading,
amber colour deepening as liquid rises. The moment the drink becomes visibly luxurious.

COLOUR PALETTE
Amber / deep gold spirit; crystal clear ice; dark bar top; warm golden ambient
light; accent of citrus yellow or orange.

MOOD AND PACING
Sophisticated, moody, craft-focused, rituals matter. Slow-paced and intentional.
The craft of bartending is visible. Warm and slightly mysterious.

PARAMETERS
Model: seedance_2_0
Duration: 8–12 s
Aspect ratio: 9:16 or 16:9
Resolution: 1080p
Genre: noir
```

## Example 5: Bakery — Croissant Reveal

```
SCENE SETUP
Warm bakery setting at golden hour. Marble or wood pastry board. Fresh-from-oven
croissants cooling. Early morning light streaming in warm and soft. Baker's hands
possible in background.

HERO ELEMENT
Single fresh croissant, warm, steam possibly still rising. Golden-brown exterior with
visible sheen. Croissant sitting whole, then being torn/broken to reveal laminated
butter layers inside.

OPENING HOOK (0–2 seconds)
Camera tight on whole croissant in detail. Golden-brown colour prominent. Steam
visibly rising. Camera slow-push toward the croissant. By second 2, hands enter
frame ready to tear.

PRIMARY MOVEMENTS
1. Hands positioning around croissant (fingers ready)
2. Tear beginning (tension, not a snap)
3. Croissant separating into two halves
4. Butter layers revealed and visible
5. Steam releasing from interior
6. Cross-section showing lamination detail
7. Crumb structure visible and flaky

CAMERA WORK
Start tight on whole croissant (golden exterior). Slight push-in as tear begins
(anticipation). Slow push-in through tear moment (focus on layers). Macro on exposed
interior. Extreme close-up on crumb structure.

LIGHTING APPROACH
Warm golden-hour window light (primary source). Backlight for steam (glows
golden/silver). Side light on croissant exterior showing texture and sheen. Light
filling interior layers as they're revealed. Warm colour temp throughout (3000 K+).

SOUND DESIGN
Subtle tear/crack sound as croissant separates (organic, not loud). Possible crumb
crackle as layers separate. Steam release (whisper of hot air). Ambient bakery: oven
faint, background music soft.

FOOD STYLING NOTES
Croissant fresh and warm (steam key). Exterior golden-brown even colour with slight
butter sheen. Lamination visible (distinct layers, not blended). Crumb structure flaky
and open. Butter colour visible in layers (pale yellow). Cut surface still steaming
and glistening.

CLOSEUP MOMENTS
Macro on golden exterior texture. Macro on steam rising. Macro on butter layer detail.
Macro on crumb structure. Macro on tear moment showing shred of dough.

THE MONEY SHOT
Croissant fully torn open, both halves visible showing perfect lamination with
distinct butter layers, steam rising between the halves, backlit and glowing. Warm
light showing every detail of the flaky crumb.

COLOUR PALETTE
Golden browns, butter yellows, pale cream interior, warm golden ambient light,
white marble or wood surface.

MOOD AND PACING
Warm, intimate, craft, comfort, morning ritual, indulgence, luxury in simplicity.
Slow-paced. This is about appreciating the making of the croissant (technique visible
in the layers).

PARAMETERS
Model: seedance_2_0
Duration: 8–12 s
Aspect ratio: 9:16 or 1:1
Resolution: 1080p
Genre: drama
```

## Food Category Playbook

| Category | Primary hook | Lighting approach | Genre suggestion |
|---|---|---|---|
| Gourmet / fine dining | Sauce Pour, Steam Rising, Knife Cutting | Warm key + backlight for sauce | `drama` |
| Fast food / casual | Cheese Pull, Sizzling on Hot Surface | Bright warm, saturated | `action` |
| Baked goods / pastry | Bread Tear, Knife Cutting | Warm window light | `drama` |
| Coffee / café | Steam Rising, Ingredient Cascade | Warm diffused window | `drama` |
| Cocktails / spirits | Bubbles Rising, Juice Drizzle | Moody bar with backlit bottles | `noir` |
| Smoothies / juice | Ingredient Cascade, Bubbles Rising | Natural bright | `auto` |
| Asian cuisine | Sizzling, Steam Rising | High-contrast warm | `epic` or `auto` |
| BBQ / grilling | Sizzling, Flame Torch | Dramatic side + backlight | `epic` |
| Desserts / sweets | Chocolate Break, Sauce Pour, Ice Cream Scoop | Dramatic or warm | `drama` |
| Health food / salads | Ingredient Cascade, Oil Drizzle | Natural bright window | `auto` |
