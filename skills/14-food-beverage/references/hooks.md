## Why the First 2 Seconds Matter

Viewers decide in 2 seconds whether to keep watching. For food and beverage video,
the opening moment must trigger appetite and curiosity. Start the hook *mid-action*
— do not show setup; show the compelling moment itself. Every hook below describes
an action to write into the Higgsfield prompt's opening instruction.

---

## 12 Proven Food and Beverage Hooks

### 1. Cheese Pull / Stretch

Melted cheese being pulled from a slice, pizza, burger, or grilled cheese. Capture
the stretch with dramatic slow-motion. The ooze is irresistible.

**Prompt phrasing:** "Camera at burger side angle. Top bun being slowly lifted away.
Melted cheese stretches downward, reaching maximum elasticity — strand still
connecting patty to bun as bun lifts. Backlight glints off the cheese. Sizzle sound."  
**Best for:** Burgers, pizza, grilled cheese, pasta, quesadillas

---

### 2. Slow-Motion Sauce Pour

Viscous sauce (béarnaise, chocolate, caramel, pesto) poured over protein or dessert.
Watch it cascade and pool. Backlight for glow.

**Prompt phrasing:** "Spoon of sauce held overhead. Sauce drizzled in slow-motion
onto the plate in a deliberate, artistic line. Backlit to show the glossy, silky
sauce catch light. Steam gently rising from the warm sauce."  
**Best for:** Fine dining, desserts, pasta, steaks

---

### 3. Steam Rising from Hot Dish

Fresh pasta being plated, soup ladle lifting with vapour, just-grilled meat releasing
steam. Steam signals freshness and heat. Backlight it.

**Prompt phrasing:** "Camera tight on dish; dense white steam rising and backlit by
warm golden light, steam illuminated like glowing gold, macro detail of steam wisps."  
**Best for:** Soups, noodles, hot beverages, fresh-baked goods

---

### 4. Knife Cutting with Satisfying Crunch

Blade hitting fresh vegetables, crust, or chocolate. The *sound* plus the *visual*
makes mouths water. Macro angle shows the cut surface.

**Prompt phrasing:** "Knife cutting cleanly through multi-layer cake in one smooth
motion, revealing distinct interior layers, crumb detail visible, slow push-in camera
as slice separates, interior texture in sharp focus."  
**Best for:** Baked goods, fresh vegetables, chocolate, meats

---

### 5. Chocolate Break / Snap

Chocolate bar or thick chocolate cake broken in half, revealing the smooth interior
or gooey centre. Dramatic lighting on the break.

**Prompt phrasing:** "Hands breaking chocolate bar in two, the clean snap revealing
glossy smooth interior, dramatic side lighting showing the facets of broken chocolate,
slow-motion capture."  
**Best for:** Desserts, confectionery, brownies, ganache cakes

---

### 6. Ice Cream Scoop with Drip

Warm spoon creating a scoop, ice cream softening and dripping onto waffle cone or
bowl. The melt is the money shot.

**Prompt phrasing:** "Warm spoon pressing into ice cream, surface yielding, scoop
forming and lifting away, soft drip falling from the edge, macro lens showing texture
and melt."  
**Best for:** Ice cream, gelato, sorbet, frozen desserts

---

### 7. Sizzling on Hot Surface

Protein or vegetable hitting a screaming-hot pan. The sizzle + steam + colour change
happens in seconds. Pure appetite trigger.

**Prompt phrasing:** "Protein or vegetables hitting screaming-hot pan, immediate
sizzle and steam, colour change from raw to seared visible, oil jumping, flames
potentially visible, close-up of the sear surface and browning."  
**Best for:** Steaks, fish, vegetables, stir-fry, BBQ

---

### 8. Bubbles Rising in Drink

Carbonation in beer, soda, or champagne; bubble foam forming; fizz erupting. Backlit
for luminosity. Hypnotic.

**Prompt phrasing:** "Crystal glass of carbonated beverage, bubbles rising from
base to surface, backlit by warm golden light so bubbles glow, slow-motion capture
of fizz and foam forming at the surface."  
**Best for:** Beer, champagne, sparkling water, soda, cocktails

---

### 9. Ingredient Cascade / Rain

Seasoning sprinkled over food, flour dusted onto dough, garnish scattered, herbs
rained from above. Capture the fall and land.

**Prompt phrasing:** "Fresh herbs or garnish rained down onto plated food from
above, each element falling and landing, captured in slow-motion, dust and micro-
particles visible in the light, creating a 'seasoning moment'."  
**Best for:** Salads, pasta, soups, baked goods, any finished dish

---

### 10. Flame or Torch Moment

Crème brûlée torch, flambéed dessert, or flame-kissed dish. The orange glow and
action are inherently cinematic.

**Prompt phrasing:** "Kitchen torch applied to crème brûlée surface, sugar caramelising
and turning amber, flame dancing and crackling, backlit smoke wisping, macro on
the caramelising sugar crust forming."  
**Best for:** Crème brûlée, flambé desserts, flame-grilled proteins

---

### 11. Juice or Oil Drizzle

High-quality olive oil, balsamic, or fresh citrus juice drizzled over plated food.
The pooling and shine.

**Prompt phrasing:** "Premium olive oil drizzled from height onto finished dish in
a slow deliberate line, liquid spreading and pooling, sheen catching light, creating
an oil slick effect that signals luxury and quality."  
**Best for:** Mediterranean dishes, salads, cheese boards, desserts

---

### 12. Bread Tear / Crumb Reveal

Tearing into fresh bread, croissant, or bagel to show the interior crumb structure
and steam. Intimacy plus appetite.

**Prompt phrasing:** "Hands tearing into fresh croissant, interior exposed, steam
releasing from the warm crumb, tear texture visible — shaggy or laminated layers —
crumb detail in sharp focus, warmth visible."  
**Best for:** Bread, croissants, pastries, rolls, bagels

## Hook Selection by Content Type

| Content type | Recommended hooks |
|---|---|
| Fine dining / restaurant promo | Sauce Pour, Steam Rising, Ingredient Cascade, Knife Cutting |
| Fast food / casual | Cheese Pull, Sizzling on Hot Surface, Flame Torch |
| Baked goods / pastry | Bread Tear, Knife Cutting, Chocolate Break |
| Beverages — coffee / café | Steam Rising, Ingredient Cascade (coffee beans) |
| Beverages — cocktails / spirits | Bubbles Rising, Juice or Oil Drizzle (citrus twist) |
| Beverages — smoothies / juice | Ingredient Cascade, Bubbles Rising |
| Desserts and sweets | Chocolate Break, Ice Cream Scoop, Flame Torch, Sauce Pour |
| BBQ and grilling | Sizzling on Hot Surface, Flame Torch, Knife Cutting |
| Health food / salads | Ingredient Cascade, Juice or Oil Drizzle |
