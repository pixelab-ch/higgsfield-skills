# Fight Scene Hook Framework

The 2-second hook framework for fight scene and action sequence video prompts.
The opening 2 seconds of a fight must GRAB. These hooks ignite engagement immediately.

**Rule:** Lead with your hook in the first sentence of your prompt. The hook must be
specific, visceral, and visually arresting — not a setup that eases into action.

---

## 10+ Essential Fight Hooks

### 1. Mid-Action Freeze Frame
Cut directly into the apex of a strike — fist or blade mid-impact, combatants at
maximum extension, muscles coiled. Hold for 0.5 seconds before explosive continuation.

**Prompt template:** "CUT INTO: a fist frozen mid-impact against [opponent's face/torso],
both combatants at maximum extension, muscles coiled, dust suspended in air. Hold 0.5
seconds. Then: time resumes, explosive continuation."

---

### 2. Weapon Clash with Spark Explosion
Two blades (or weapons) collide dead-center screen. Orange sparks burst radially.
Camera pulls back slightly as the shockwave rings out.

**Prompt template:** "Two blades collide center-screen with violent impact. Orange sparks
burst radially outward. Camera pulls back slightly as shockwave rings through the air.
Both combatants' arms strain against each other's blade."

---

### 3. Character Charging Directly at Camera
Aggressor sprints toward the camera with wild intensity, limbs extended. Camera shakes
slightly from proximity. Stop just before impact, then cut to the actual fight.

**Prompt template:** "Aggressor sprints DIRECTLY at camera at full sprint, wild intensity
in their eyes, limbs extending toward lens. Camera shakes from proximity. Hard stop just
before impact — then cut to fight."

---

### 4. Slow-Motion Punch Impact
A perfectly centered punch connects with face/body in slow motion (0.25x speed). Show
the moment of contact: skin deforming, head whipping back, eyes widening in shock.

**Prompt template:** "Slow-motion (0.25x speed): punch connects with [face/body], skin
deforming at contact point, head whipping backward, eyes wide in shock. Sweat beads fly
from centrifugal force."

---

### 5. Shockwave from Ground Slam
Combatant slams fist or body into earth. A visible shockwave radiates outward, kicking
up dust in a perfect circle. Secondary character staggers backward.

**Prompt template:** "[Combatant] slams [fist/body] into earth. Visible shockwave
radiates outward in perfect circle, kicking up dust. Secondary character staggers
backward, arms thrown up for balance."

---

### 6. Blade Unsheathing with Metallic Flash
Draw a katana, nodachi, or longsword. The blade catches light with a brilliant flash.
The metallic ring of steel implied by visual energy.

**Prompt template:** "[Character] draws [katana/longsword] from sheath in one explosive
motion. Blade catches light with brilliant flash illuminating face and surrounding area.
Opponent's eyes narrow in response."

---

### 7. Aerial Kick Descending
Combatant launches vertically, leg extended in a perfect axe kick or flying kick.
They descend toward opponent at camera's eye level. Ground impact creates visible dust
cloud on contact.

**Prompt template:** "Combatant seen from below, suspended mid-air at apex of jump, leg
extended in perfect axe kick, descending toward opponent. Ground-level camera. Impact
creates visible dust cloud explosion."

---

### 8. Dual-Wield Weapon Twirl
Character spins twin weapons (swords, staffs, nunchaku) in rapid succession — blades
blur. Stops in power stance, weapons crossing the chest.

**Prompt template:** "[Character] spins twin [weapons] in rapid succession — blades blur
into visual arcs. Motion slows. They stop in power stance, [weapons] crossing the chest,
eyes locked on opponent."

---

### 9. Rope/Chain Snap
Whip or chain weapon snaps through frame with violent acceleration. Visual crack energy
radiates from contact point. Can precede grapple or wrapping move.

**Prompt template:** "Whip cracks violently through frame from off-screen. Visual energy
radiates from contact point. Opponent stumbles backward from the force. [Character]
advances."

---

### 10. Opponent Stumble/Dodge Backward
One combatant charges; the other pivots explosively and sidesteps. Attacker's momentum
carries them past the camera, creating dynamic depth. Chase-cam follows the dodge.

**Prompt template:** "Attacker charges. Defender pivots explosively, sidesteps with
perfect timing. Attacker's momentum carries them PAST camera, frame blurring. Camera
follows Defender's dodge with whip-pan — repositioning for next exchange."

---

## Hook Pairing Guide

| Fight Type | Best Hooks | Notes |
|------------|-----------|-------|
| **Martial arts duel** | Aerial Kick, Slow-Motion Punch, Mid-Action Freeze | Lead with technique precision |
| **Sword / weapon combat** | Blade Unsheathing, Weapon Clash with Sparks, Dual-Wield Twirl | Lead with weapon drama |
| **Action chase / street fight** | Character Charging, Opponent Stumble/Dodge, Ground Slam | Lead with momentum and movement |
| **Fantasy / elemental** | Ground Slam (elemental variant), Weapon Clash (energy variant), Aerial Kick | Lead with visual spectacle |
| **Superhero battle** | Ground Slam, Slow-Motion, Character Charging | Scale and power — exaggerate impact |

## Hook Escalation

After the hook, maintain escalation:
- Hook establishes the register (how intense, how stylized)
- Exchange 1 develops the hook's promise
- Climax exceeds the hook — the biggest moment is at the end, not the beginning

Never peak at the hook. The hook is the promise; the rest of the prompt is the delivery.
