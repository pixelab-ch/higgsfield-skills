## Why the First 2 Seconds Matter

The first 2 seconds determine if viewers scroll past or watch to purchase. The hook
must instantly signal "premium", "new", or "this solves my problem". Start mid-action
— do not show setup, show the compelling moment itself. Every hook below is a pattern
to open a Higgsfield prompt.

---

## 12 Proven E-Commerce Hooks

### Hook 1: Product Drop with Dramatic Impact

Dark minimal background. Product descends with motion blur and dramatic lighting shift.

**Why it works:** Instantly signals "premium" and "new arrival."  
**Prompt phrasing:** "Dark matte background. Product descends into frame with motion
blur, catching dramatic side-light mid-drop. Soft glow emanates as it lands centre
frame."  
**Best for:** Luxury watches, electronics, premium skincare, jewelry

---

### Hook 2: Satisfying Texture ASMR Close-Up

Macro shot of product texture — fabric weave, metal grain, skin texture.

**Why it works:** Triggers sensory engagement. Viewers pause to watch satisfying
detail.  
**Prompt phrasing:** "Extreme macro close-up of product texture — fabric weave or
metal surface. Camera slow-pushes in, revealing material quality in high detail.
Warm side-lighting showing every detail."  
**Best for:** Textiles, leather goods, beauty, skincare

---

### Hook 3: Before/After Transformation

Split-screen or quick transition showing problem state → solved state.

**Why it works:** Instantly communicates benefit. Answers "what's in it for me?"  
**Prompt phrasing:** "Dull/problem state shown in first second. Clean cut to radiant/
solved state. Product revealed as the bridge between them."  
**Best for:** Skincare, cleaning products, organisation products, fitness gear

---

### Hook 4: Stop Scrolling Direct Address

Model or creator looks directly at camera with hand gesture toward camera.

**Why it works:** Breaks the fourth wall. Feels personal and urgent.  
**Prompt phrasing:** "Person looks directly at camera, hand raised in 'stop' gesture,
slight smile, confident expression."  
**Best for:** Social-first DTC brands, impulse purchases, lifestyle products

---

### Hook 5: Unboxing Reveal

Hands open premium packaging. Product emerges into light.

**Why it works:** Taps into unboxing fascination. Shows quality through packaging.  
**Prompt phrasing:** "Premium packaging being carefully opened. Product emerges from
tissue paper into warm studio light — the reveal is the hook."  
**Best for:** Electronics, luxury goods, subscription boxes, gift products

---

### Hook 6: Colour/Variant Cascade

Products line up in quick succession, rotating to show colour or variant range.

**Why it works:** Shows range. Viewers looking for "their colour" stay watching.  
**Prompt phrasing:** "Products arranged in line, rotating quickly through colour
variants, each catching studio light in sequence."  
**Best for:** Fashion, cosmetics, phone cases, accessories

---

### Hook 7: Ingredient Explosion

Product ingredients or components splinter outward — visual, dynamic, energetic.

**Why it works:** Communicates "natural" or "premium ingredients" visually.  
**Prompt phrasing:** "Key product ingredients explode outward from the product
centre, each ingredient visible mid-flight, particles catching warm light, then
re-gather."  
**Best for:** Skincare, supplements, food/beverage products

---

### Hook 8: Problem-Solution Snap

Text overlay "Problem:" → quick cut → "Solution:" with product reveal.

**Why it works:** Direct, solution-focused. No fluff.  
**Prompt phrasing:** "Text overlay 'Problem:' fades in over problem visual. Hard cut
to 'Solution:' with product emerging from dark background."  
**Best for:** Fitness, home organisation, productivity tools, health products

---

### Hook 9: In-Hand Usage Tease

Hands holding product, demonstrating the key feature in 2 seconds.

**Why it works:** Shows context immediately — "I see how this fits into my life."  
**Prompt phrasing:** "Hands hold product demonstrating its signature feature —
drop-resistance, flexibility, transformation — in first 2 seconds."  
**Best for:** Phone accessories, tools, safety products, sports gear

---

### Hook 10: Lifestyle Aspiration Flash

Quick cut to aspirational lifestyle scene with product integrated naturally.

**Why it works:** Sells the life, not the product. Emotional hook.  
**Prompt phrasing:** "Morning sunlight, person in aspirational lifestyle setting
with product naturally integrated — not posed, genuine lifestyle moment."  
**Best for:** Fashion, wellness, home goods, coffee/beverage

---

### Hook 11: Scarcity/Urgency Signal

Text overlay "Limited Drop" or "Only 500 Made" with product glow.

**Why it works:** FOMO drives click-through and add-to-cart.  
**Prompt phrasing:** "Product with animated 'LIMITED DROP' text glowing around it,
urgency animation pulsing, premium product in warm studio light."  
**Best for:** Limited editions, flash sales, launch campaigns

---

### Hook 12: Creator Reaction

Creator unboxes with genuine surprise or delight — "oh my god" moment.

**Why it works:** Authentic emotional response is contagious.  
**Prompt phrasing:** "Creator's expression of genuine surprise and delight as
product is revealed, eyes wide, then direct look at camera."  
**Best for:** Beauty, lifestyle, gifts, subscription boxes

## Hook Selection by Product Category

| Product category | Recommended hooks |
|---|---|
| Fashion and apparel | Colour/Variant Cascade, In-Hand Usage Tease, Lifestyle Flash |
| Beauty and skincare | Satisfying Texture, Before/After, Ingredient Explosion |
| Electronics and gadgets | Product Drop, Unboxing Reveal, Problem-Solution Snap |
| Food and beverage | Ingredient Explosion, Satisfying Texture, Lifestyle Flash |
| Jewelry and accessories | Product Drop, Satisfying Texture, Lifestyle Flash |
| Home and furniture | Lifestyle Aspiration Flash, Before/After Transformation |
| Fitness and wellness | Before/After, Problem-Solution Snap, In-Hand Usage |
| Toys and gaming | Unboxing Reveal, Creator Reaction |
| Pet products | Creator Reaction, Lifestyle Aspiration Flash |
| Automotive accessories | Problem-Solution Snap, Before/After Transformation |
