# Product-360 Hook Framework

The first 2 seconds determine if viewers engage. These hooks trigger immediate visual
interest and communicate luxury, innovation, or desirability.

---

## 12 Hook Styles

### 1. Particle Materialization
Product emerges from a cloud of particles or dust, slowly solidifying. Perfect for tech,
jewelry, luxury goods.

`"Product materializes from golden particles, coalescing from mist into perfect form."`

---

### 2. Dramatic Spotlight Snap
Single harsh spotlight suddenly illuminates the product in darkness, revealing hero details
instantly. Luxury watch and jewelry staple.

`"Spotlight snaps on, product gleams in sharp light, shadow recedes."`

---

### 3. Crash and Shockwave
Product crashes down from above with impact, creating subtle dust and motion blur. Implies
robustness, drama, premium engineering.

`"Product crashes onto surface, shockwave ripples outward, comes to rest."`

---

### 4. Unwrap and Peel Reveal
Packaging peels away, tissue unfolds, protective layer retracts. Unboxing energy.

`"Protective wrapping peels back, revealing pristine product beneath."`

---

### 5. Impossible-Angle Macro-to-Wide
Start extreme close-up (micro detail, texture, material), then pull out to full product
view. Creates "wow" moment.

`"Macro shot of leather stitching pulls back, revealing full luxury bag."`

---

### 6. Self-Assembly
Product components float, snap into place, assemble themselves with mechanical precision.
Tech and modular products.

`"Modular segments float and lock together, forming complete device."`

---

### 7. Exploded View Snap-Together
All parts visible and separated, then snap together with satisfying mechanical sound.
Complexity-to-simplicity narrative.

`"All components visible in space, rapidly snap into tight assembly."`

---

### 8. Liquid Pour and Transparency Bloom
Transparent product fills with color, liquid component appears, frosting effect. Beauty
and beverage products.

`"Perfume fills bottle with color, liquid settles, light refracts through glass."`

---

### 9. Slow-Motion Lift
Product gracefully lifts from surface in slow motion, floating into rotation. Elegant,
premium, weightless.

`"Product slowly lifts from surface, floating into smooth rotation."`

---

### 10. Spin-to-Reveal
Half rotation (180°) that reveals hidden side detail, embossing, or branding. Efficient
surprise.

`"Half-rotation reveals embossed detail on back panel."`

---

### 11. Zoom and Pan Transition
Camera zooms into texture, then pans to full product. Material storytelling opener.

`"Camera dives into brushed metal surface, pulls back to reveal entire watch."`

---

### 12. Halo Light Flare
Gorgeous light reflection or flare appears around product, emphasizing shine and luxury.
Jewelry default.

`"Brilliant light flare cascades across diamond surface, illuminating facets."`

---

## Matching Hook to Category

| Category | Recommended hooks |
|----------|------------------|
| Jewelry / Watches | Dramatic Spotlight Snap, Halo Light Flare, Particle Materialization |
| Luxury goods (bags, cosmetics) | Slow-Motion Lift, Unwrap/Peel, Particle Materialization |
| Tech / Electronics | Self-Assembly, Exploded View, Zoom and Pan |
| Footwear | Crash and Shockwave, Macro-to-Wide, Spin-to-Reveal |
| Automotive | Crash and Shockwave, Dramatic Spotlight, Macro-to-Wide |
| Beauty / Fragrance | Liquid Pour, Halo Light Flare, Slow-Motion Lift |
| Food packaging | Unwrap/Peel, Liquid Pour, Macro-to-Wide |

---

## Advanced Hook Stacking

Combine two compatible hooks for layered impact. Order: immediate attention-grab (0–0.5 s)
followed by reveal (0.5–2 s).

| Primary hook | Compatible secondary |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Dramatic Spotlight Snap | Halo Light Flare |
| Crash and Shockwave | Macro-to-Wide |
| Particle Materialization | Slow-Motion Lift |
| Unwrap/Peel | Spin-to-Reveal |

**Rule:** Do not stack more than two hooks — complexity beyond two competes with the
product rather than serving it.
