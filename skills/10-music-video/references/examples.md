## Master Template

Use this as the starting point. Fill in each section based on the song and vision.

```
[Song Title] - Music Video Prompt

AUDIO: [user audio track — uploaded via media_upload → media_confirm, role: audio]

VIDEO STRATEGY: [Performance / Narrative / Abstract]

OVERALL AESTHETIC:
Genre-visual language: [e.g., "urban hip-hop with neon accents," "ethereal pop with bright candy colors"]
Color palette: [primary colors]
Lighting style: [e.g., "hard shadows," "natural light," "neon glow"]
Camera movement priority: [e.g., "dynamic and fast," "slow and intimate," "orbital and geometric"]

VERSE 1 (0:00-0:25):
Energy level: [low / medium / high]
Visual focus: [performer / scene / abstract elements]
Key visual elements: [3-5 specific visual ideas]
Beat synchronization: "At [timing], [visual action] on [beat reference]"

CHORUS (0:25-0:40):
Energy level: [medium / high / maximum]
Visual hook: [describe the main 2-second visual hook]
Repetition strategy: "This visual repeats every 4 bars with [variation]"
Color / lighting: [specific changes or shifts]

BRIDGE (0:40-0:50):
Shift from chorus: [what changes]
Visual strategy: [new visual / continuation / subtle variation]
Key moment: "At [timing], [dramatic visual change]"

FINAL CHORUS / DROP (0:50-1:05):
Intensity level: [maximum / explosive]
Visual peak: [most impactful visual moment]
Layering: "Build from [starting visual] to [peak visual]"

OUTRO (1:05-end):
Energy trajectory: [fading / explosive / resolution]
Final image: [what viewers see last]

TECHNICAL NOTES:
- Emphasis on beat drops at [timings]
- Fast cut rate during [section] for rhythmic energy
- Particle / motion intensity should peak at [timing]
- Color transitions should happen at [harmonic shifts]
```

## Example 1 — Hip-Hop Performance Video

```
Song Title: "Crown" (Hip-Hop Track)
AUDIO: [user track — uploaded via media_upload → media_confirm, role: audio]
VIDEO STRATEGY: Performance-focused, showcasing artist confidence and urban energy

SETTING & AESTHETIC:
Location: Dark warehouse with industrial elements (metal, concrete, exposed beams)
Lighting: Hard directional spotlights, deep shadows, occasional neon accents (gold, red)
Color Palette: Gold, red, blacks, deep purples
Overall Tone: Gritty, confident, powerful swagger

OPENING (0:00-0:08):
Dark warehouse interior. Minimal lighting. Single spotlight illuminates ground.
Artist enters from left, steps into spotlight with confident stride.
At 0:08 (first beat drop), harsh spotlight hits artist's face directly.

VERSE 1 (0:08-0:25):
Tight close-up on artist's face during first line of verse.
Every 4 bars: cut to wide shot showing full artist in spotlight, dark warehouse background.
Hard shadows across face, rim-lighting creating edge.
Slow orbital camera movement around artist, one full orbit per 8 bars.
Gold neon sign flickers in background, creating irregular light accents.

PRE-CHORUS BUILD (0:25-0:32):
Cut rate increases: wide angle and tight face close-ups alternating every 2 seconds.
Camera push-in on artist, arriving at extreme close-up of face by 0:32.
Second spotlight activates, creating cross-light effect on face.

CHORUS (0:32-0:45):
VISUAL HOOK: Red light floods the entire warehouse, creating shadow of artist against back wall.
Artist moves more dynamically, reflecting confidence of vocal performance.
Rapid cuts between: profile shots, front-facing close-ups, full-body shots.
Every kick drum: cut to new angle. Every snare hit: color flash (gold to red).

VERSE 2 (0:45-1:05):
Return to single spotlight. Another figure appears in background, visual tension.
Camera orbit continues, now includes wider pulls to show both figures.
At 1:00 (minor emotional peak): push-in on artist's eyes, intensity.

FINAL CHORUS / CLIMAX (1:05-1:25):
All lights activate: red, gold, white spotlights create controlled lighting chaos.
Artist center frame, moving with full confidence. Maximum frequency cuts.
At 1:15 (final drop): ultra-wide shot showing full warehouse in gold and red,
artist as silhouette in center.

OUTRO (1:25-end):
Lights cut to black except single spotlight on artist.
Artist strikes final pose. Fade to black as music fades.

TECHNICAL NOTES:
- Chorus: every kick = visual cut / color flash
- Hard directional lighting throughout, avoid flat/even light
- Neon accents should feel environmental, not choreographed
```

## Example 2 — Emotional Pop Ballad

```
Song Title: "Without You" (Pop Ballad)
AUDIO: [user track — uploaded via media_upload → media_confirm, role: audio]
VIDEO STRATEGY: Narrative — character dealing with absence and acceptance

SETTING & AESTHETIC:
Location: Interior apartment (warm, personal) and exterior city (cool, lonely)
Lighting: Warm interior vs cool exterior. Interior: candlelight and window light. Exterior: rain and neon.
Color Palette: Interior = warm gold and amber; Exterior = cool blue and grey
Mood Arc: From warm memory → cold reality → acceptance

VERSE 1 (0:00-0:25):
Warm apartment interior. Close-up on character sitting alone with coffee.
Slow push-in on face. Soft, golden light. Film grain for warmth and nostalgia.
Objects in frame suggest a shared space that's now one person's. Subtle detail.
Camera moves slowly, intimately. No fast cuts. Let the emptiness breathe.

CHORUS (0:25-0:45):
VISUAL HOOK: Cut to exterior — character walking in rain. Cool, neon-lit street. Color drains.
Fast cuts between: character walking, remembered moments, empty apartment objects.
Each cut synced to emotional peaks in the vocal. Return to face during held notes.
Rain effect synced to percussion — each raindrop emphasizes beat.

BRIDGE (1:10-1:25):
Character stops walking. Looks up at rain. Static shot — no camera movement.
Silence in motion. Still frame. Emotional peak.
Slow pull-back reveals the full empty street. Character small in frame.

FINAL CHORUS (1:25-1:45):
Character returns inside. Warm light returns. Subtle shift in expression: acceptance.
Cut rate slows. Camera holds wider, letting the emotion settle.
Final image: character and empty chair. Held.

OUTRO (1:45-end):
Lights slowly warm further. Simple fade. Resolve.
```

## Example 3 — EDM Abstract Visualizer

```
Song Title: "Singularity" (Progressive House / EDM)
AUDIO: [user track — uploaded via media_upload → media_confirm, role: audio]
VIDEO STRATEGY: Abstract — pure visual expression responding to audio

SETTING & AESTHETIC:
Environment: Deep space / abstract void. No performer. Pure geometry and light.
Color Palette: Deep black background; neon cyan, magenta, and gold geometry.
Movement: All visual elements respond directly to audio frequency content.

INTRO (0:00-0:20):
Single point of light in center of black void. Minimal.
Light slowly pulses with the BPM of the song.
Every 4 bars, one new neon geometric line appears, rotating slowly.
By 0:20, 4-5 geometric lines in slow orbit.

PRE-DROP BUILD (0:20-0:40):
Geometric shapes accelerate rotation speed — matching increase in track energy.
Colors shift from single cyan → add magenta → add gold.
Zoom slowly toward the center convergence point.
Particle density increases with each layer of drums added.

DROP (0:40-1:00):
VISUAL PEAK: All geometry converges to center and EXPLODES outward.
Particles burst radially in all three colors simultaneously.
Cut rate maximizes — each kick drum triggers a new geometric burst.
Neon laser beams appear, rotating at tempo.
Camera (POV) zooms forward into the geometric field at breakneck speed.

BREAKDOWN (1:00-1:20):
Geometry slows. Colors cool to deep blue. Particle density drops.
Single sine wave appears, representing the bass line.
Camera pulls back slowly.

FINAL DROP (1:20-1:45):
Maximum intensity. All techniques combined simultaneously.
Fractal patterns layer over the geometry. Particles reach maximum density.
Color cycles through full spectrum every 8 bars.
Final beat: freeze on geometric explosion. Hold.

OUTRO (1:45-end):
Reverse big bang — particles slowly retract to center point. Fade to black.
```

## Example 4 — R&B Narrative Video

```
Song Title: "Golden Hour" (R&B / Neo-Soul)
AUDIO: [user track — uploaded via media_upload → media_confirm, role: audio]
VIDEO STRATEGY: Narrative — a relationship at its peak and beginning to change

SETTING & AESTHETIC:
Locations: Rooftop at golden hour → interior dimly lit room → empty rooftop at night
Lighting: Golden hour warmth → transitional glow → blue-hour cool
Color Palette: Warm amber and gold → transitional → deep teal and indigo

VERSE 1 (0:00-0:25):
Rooftop at golden hour. Two people, close but not quite touching.
Camera moves slowly and intimately — close-ups of hands, faces, hair.
All warmth: gold, amber, copper tones. Light flatters both subjects.
Slow dolly push-in during first vocal hook.

CHORUS (0:25-0:45):
VISUAL HOOK: Camera pulls back slowly to reveal the full rooftop and golden horizon.
Subjects move closer as visual space expands. Beautiful contrast.
Warm color grade intensifies. Motion synced to melody, not percussion.
Close-up of face on each long held vocal note.

VERSE 2 (0:45-1:10):
Interior. Dim, slightly cooler. Something has shifted.
Same proximity, but the energy is different. Space between them.
Color grade moves toward teal. Shadows deepen.
Camera movement is more uncertain — subtle handheld quality.

BRIDGE (1:10-1:25):
Empty rooftop at night. One person. Blue-hour light. Cool.
Static frame. Character alone. Facing the city.
Pull-back revealing the scale of the space now that it's just one.

FINAL CHORUS (1:25-1:45):
Intercut between golden hour memory and blue-hour present.
Each cut synced to vocal phrase. Contrast between warm/cool emphasizes emotional distance.
Final image: golden hour still, as if preserving the memory.
```

## Example 5 — K-Pop Choreography Showcase

```
Song Title: "Frequencies" (K-Pop)
AUDIO: [user track — uploaded via media_upload → media_confirm, role: audio]
VIDEO STRATEGY: Performance — synchronized choreography with high visual energy

SETTING & AESTHETIC:
Locations: Geometric stage set → neon environment → outdoor rooftop finale
Lighting: High contrast, multiple light sources, color shifting per section
Color Palette: Verse = sleek black/white; Chorus = vibrant cyan and pink; Bridge = neon green

VERSE 1 (0:00-0:20):
5 performers, sleek black and white outfits, geometric stage set.
Synchronized formation: V-shape pointing at camera. Deliberate, precise movement.
Camera: slow push-in, holding wide to show full formation.
Cuts: slow — one cut every 8 bars, letting formation hold.

PRE-CHORUS (0:20-0:30):
Formation shifts. Performers spread into line. Color behind them shifts to hot pink.
Cut rate increases. Start alternating between wide formation and individual close-ups.
Each performer gets a 1-2 second close-up before chorus hits.

CHORUS (0:30-0:45):
VISUAL HOOK: Wide shot of full choreography synchronized perfectly with beat drop.
Rapid cuts — each snare hit = new angle (wide, then individual, then wide).
Outfits shift color in the light (subtle costume design). Energy: maximum.
Color bursts (cyan, pink, gold) on every 4th beat.

BRIDGE (1:10-1:25):
Location shift: neon green environment. Outfit change. Slower, more dramatic movement.
Spotlight on lead performer. Others in softer light behind.
Camera orbits lead performer slowly for 8 bars.

FINAL CHORUS (1:25-1:45):
Rooftop, outdoor location. Daylight with dramatic cloud background.
Highest energy choreography. Camera holds wider to show scale.
Final formation: all performers in line, synchronized final move.
Freeze-frame on the peak beat. Hold. Then slow fade as music ends.

TECHNICAL NOTES:
- Every snare = visual cut during chorus
- Wide shots must show full group choreography, not just faces
- Outfit color should shift subtly with lighting changes (consistent design)
```

## Lyric Video Techniques

For lyric video requests, text animation syncs visuals to vocals.

| Technique | Description | Best for |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| Word-by-Word | Each word appears as it's sung (0.2 s fade-in, 0.5 s hold, fade-out) | Flowing, line-by-line feeling |
| Line-Based | Full line appears at start of phrase, slides out when next begins | Clean, readable presentation |
| Syllable-by-Syllable | Each syllable appears as sung (0.1–0.2 s gaps for fast rap) | Hip-hop, fast-paced sections |
| Lyric Echo / Delay | Main lyric appears, then ghost version offset 0.3 s later | Emotional / atmospheric moments |
| Color-Coded Lyrics | Different vocal layers in different colors | Multi-layered vocal sections |
| Kinetic Typography | Words spin, rotate, grow, bounce as they appear | Hook words, emotional peaks |
| Background Visuals | Animated background behind readable text | Full visualizer experience |

**Kinetic typography rule:** Important or repeated words get more dramatic animations.
Rhyming words animate in similar fashion (visual pattern matching audio rhyme scheme).
