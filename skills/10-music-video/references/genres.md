## Color-Mood-Genre Quick Reference

| Genre | Primary Colors | Mood | Lighting |
|-------|---------------|------|----------|
| Hip-Hop / Rap | Gold, red, blacks, deep purple | Bold, confident, moody | Hard shadows, neon, streetlights |
| Pop | Bright saturated (pink, cyan, yellow) | Joyful, energetic, playful | Even, bright, candy-colored |
| Rock / Metal | Dark reds, blacks, oranges, grays | Intense, rebellious, dark | Spotlights, fire, smoke |
| Electronic / EDM | Neon (purple, cyan, green), white | Futuristic, energetic, abstract | Particle-heavy, laser-like |
| R&B / Soul | Warm golds, deep teals, browns, rich reds | Smooth, intimate, sensual | Warm, intimate, candlelit |
| Lo-Fi / Chill | Warm golds, desaturated pastels, vintage tones | Nostalgic, calm, relaxed | Natural, soft, film grain |
| Classical | Elegant silvers, golds, whites, nature greens | Refined, timeless, ethereal | Natural light, spotlights |
| Jazz | Deep purples, golds, blacks, smoky grays | Sophisticated, intimate, moody | Dim, intimate, rim lighting |
| Country | Golden hour orange, dusty browns, deep blues | Warm, nostalgic, open | Natural sunset/sunrise, barn light |
| K-Pop | Vibrant multi-color (rainbow, neons) | Energetic, fashionable, synchronized | High contrast, multiple light sources |

## Genre Visual Language Entries

### Hip-Hop / Rap

**Core Visual Language:**
- Urban environments: warehouses, parking lots, streets, apartments, cars
- Strong contrast: hard shadows, neon against darkness
- Swagger and confidence in camera work and performer movement
- Occasional slow-mo on attitude moments
- Gritty textures: concrete, graffiti, metal
- Color palette: gold, deep purple, red, black, occasional neon accents

**Prompt trigger words:** urban, gritty, swagger, hard-hitting, dark moody, neon accents,
street energy, flex aesthetic

**Visual hooks:**
- Performer in sharp spotlight with harsh shadows
- Quick cuts between multiple locations
- Extreme close-ups (face, chain, jewelry, tattoo)
- Camera tilts and whip-pans on beat
- Glitch effects during aggressive ad-libs

**Example:** `"Dark warehouse with one harsh spotlight. At the drop (0:32), performer steps
into light with a sharp cut, then rapid cuts between close-ups of jewelry and wide shots
of empty space, synced to the snare rhythm."`

### Pop

**Core Visual Language:**
- Bright, saturated, candy colors
- Clean, energetic choreography if performers are present
- Wide, open framing (shows off fashion, hair, makeup)
- Fast cuts during chorus, slower verse
- Fashion, makeup changes, or styling focus
- Playful camera movement: quick pans, spins, dynamic cuts

**Prompt trigger words:** bright, colorful, energetic, fashionable, dance-focused, joyful,
playful, candy-colored, pop aesthetic, high-energy cuts

**Visual hooks:**
- Chorus hits and camera spins or whips to new angle
- Color explosion at drops
- Synchronized choreography (performer + background dancers)
- Fashion / makeup moments (sparkles, fabrics close-ups)
- Confetti or light bursts on beat

**Example:** `"Bright candy-colored bedroom set. Verse 1 is slow with performer styling focus.
At 0:20, the pre-chorus builds with faster cuts. Chorus explodes with vibrant pink and cyan
lighting, synchronized dance movement, confetti bursts on every 4th beat."`

### Rock / Metal

**Core Visual Language:**
- Dark, intense, moody aesthetic
- Fire, smoke, destruction, raw energy
- Hard shadows, spotlights, strobes
- Explosive moments (crashes, fires, impacts)
- Tension building through shadows and light play
- Color palette: reds, blacks, oranges, grays, occasional electric blue

**Prompt trigger words:** dark, intense, fire, aggressive, heavy, powerful, explosive,
hard shadows, rock energy, metal aesthetic

**Visual hooks:**
- Extreme close-up of face with harsh spotlight
- Crash moment synced to hit (cymbal crash, snare crack)
- Fire or explosion on drop
- Performer headbang or head tilt synced to beat
- Rapid strobe cuts during heavy sections

**Example:** `"Dark stage with distant fire. Intro minimal with rim-lighting on performer's
silhouette. First verse builds with shadow play. At 0:35, chorus hits and fire explodes
upward on the downbeat, then rapid strobe cuts sync to snare rhythm every beat."`

### Electronic / EDM

**Core Visual Language:**
- Futuristic, neon, particle-heavy
- Abstract geometric shapes morphing and responding to music
- Laser-like light beams, neon colors (cyan, magenta, green)
- Repeating geometric patterns, tessellations
- Rapid movement synchronized to beat
- Often no performer (pure abstract), or performer as silhouette

**Prompt trigger words:** neon, futuristic, particles, geometric, lasers, abstract,
synthesizer-visual, EDM aesthetic, electronic-responsive, digital landscape

**Visual hooks:**
- Particle explosion on kick drum
- Geometry morphing to melodic line
- Neon color shift on harmonic change
- Fast recursive patterns on beat
- Tunnel / zoom effect into neon space

**Example:** `"Neon purple and cyan abstract digital landscape. On the kick drum, particles
burst outward in cyan. During the build (0:25-0:40), geometric shapes accelerate rotation
and neon colors cycle through spectrum every 8 bars. On the drop (0:40), zoom rapidly into
a tunnel of purple light with white particle rain falling at tempo."`

### R&B / Soul

**Core Visual Language:**
- Warm, intimate, sensual aesthetic
- Soft, intimate lighting (candlelight, rim lighting, warm spotlights)
- Close-ups and slow camera movement
- Emotional, genuine performance moments
- Smooth color grading with warm tones
- Often focuses on facial expression and connection

**Prompt trigger words:** warm, intimate, sensual, soul, smooth, emotional, R&B, candlelit,
warm tones, intimate performance

**Visual hooks:**
- Close-up of face during emotional moment (synced to vulnerable lyric)
- Slow push-in on performer during chorus
- Subtle light flickers emphasizing vocal moments
- Warm color shift during key emotional lines
- Camera pull-back revealing whole body at chorus peak

**Example:** `"Warm room lit by rim-light and candlelight. Close-up on performer's face
throughout verse, with subtle camera push-in as emotion builds. At 0:30 (chorus), pull back
to reveal full silhouette against warm golden light, then push back to close-up on the
emotional peak at 0:40."`

### Lo-Fi / Chill

**Core Visual Language:**
- Nostalgic, warm film grain
- Desaturated colors, vintage aesthetic
- Slow, minimal camera movement
- Mundane or peaceful scenes: bedroom, coffee shop, bookshelf, rain window
- Melancholic but peaceful mood
- Analog / VHS texture, vintage color grading

**Prompt trigger words:** nostalgic, lo-fi, chill, film grain, vintage, warm, desaturated,
calm, peaceful, analog aesthetic

**Visual hooks:**
- Slow pan across cozy scene (bookshelf, desk, window)
- Subtle color shift as beat subtly changes
- Minimal cuts, long takes
- Rain or weather effects for mood
- Slight VHS glitch or tape degradation at key moments

**Example:** `"Cozy bedroom at golden hour. Warm film grain throughout. Slow pan across shelf
of books and plants during intro. Static wide shot of window with rain during verse. At
chorus (0:20), subtle warm orange grade shift and slow push-in on a coffee cup."`

### Classical

**Core Visual Language:**
- Elegant, refined, timeless
- Natural elements or architectural beauty
- Orchestral instruments or classical musicians
- Clean, wide framing with symmetrical composition
- Natural light, spotlights, or candlelight
- Color palette: silvers, golds, natural earth tones, whites

**Prompt trigger words:** elegant, classical, refined, orchestral, timeless, architectural,
natural light, sophisticated, cinematic

**Visual hooks:**
- Tight shot of musician's hands playing (during fast passages)
- Wide shot of concert hall or nature backdrop
- Camera slowly pulling back to reveal grandeur at climax
- Synchronized camera movement to melody (push-in on high notes, pull-back on low)
- Dramatic lighting shift on key harmonic moments

**Example:** `"Classical concert hall with ornate ceiling. Tight shot of violinist's hands
during opening passage. Wide establishing shot at 0:15. During main melody, camera slowly
orbits musician. On climactic high note passage, push in dramatically while lighting
increases."`

### Jazz

**Core Visual Language:**
- Sophisticated, intimate, moody
- Jazz clubs, bars, or dimly lit venues
- Noir aesthetic: deep shadows, rim lighting, cigarette smoke
- Focus on musician skill and expression
- Improvisational energy captured through unexpected cuts
- Color palette: purples, golds, blacks, smoky grays

**Prompt trigger words:** jazz, noir, sophisticated, smoky, intimate, improvisation,
jazz club, dim lighting, moody

**Visual hooks:**
- Close-up of musician's face during expressive moment
- Sudden cut to new angle (mirroring improvisational surprises)
- Smoke or haze that shifts with energy of the playing
- Rim-lit silhouettes during quieter moments
- Dynamic but graceful camera movement during solos

**Example:** `"Jazz club, dim smoky atmosphere. Dark purple and gold color grading. Rim-lit
silhouette of saxophonist against dark background. During main solo (0:30-0:50), series of
cuts between close-ups of hands, face, and wide stage shots — each cut feels improvised,
not on strict beat."`

### Country

**Core Visual Language:**
- Warm, nostalgic, open-air aesthetic
- Landscapes, golden hour, rural settings
- Focus on authenticity, emotion, and storytelling
- Sunset/sunrise lighting, barn light, natural light through windows
- Earth tones: golden oranges, dusty browns, deep blues
- Often includes horses, fields, small-town settings

**Prompt trigger words:** country, golden hour, landscape, rural, authentic, nostalgic,
barn light, sunset, warm earth tones, storytelling

**Visual hooks:**
- Wide establishing shot of landscape during intro
- Slow pan across field or landscape
- Warm golden light on performer's face
- Silhouette against sunset at emotional peak
- Rural elements (horse, field, fence) integrated into emotional moments

**Example:** `"Golden hour landscape — field at sunset. Wide establishing shot during intro.
Verse 1: performer in center frame, warm golden backlight. Chorus: closer framing, more
direct golden light. Bridge: silhouette against deep orange sky. Final chorus: golden light
with camera held wide."`

### K-Pop

**Core Visual Language:**
- Vibrant, multi-color palette (often rainbow or neon-heavy)
- Synchronized choreography (often group or duo focus)
- Fashion and styling as primary visual element
- High-contrast lighting with multiple light sources
- Fast cuts, dynamic energy, playful camera movements
- Often shifts between multiple locations or outfit changes

**Prompt trigger words:** K-pop, vibrant, choreography, fashionable, synchronized, neon,
energetic, colorful, high-contrast, multi-color palette

**Visual hooks:**
- Sharp freeze-frame on choreography moment
- Rapid cuts in time with snare hits during chorus
- Outfit / location change on beat or section transition
- Neon-color flashes synced to drum hits
- Wide shot of group synchronized movement, then cuts to individual style moments

**Example:** `"High-energy K-pop aesthetic with vibrant neon colors. Verse 1: performers in
sleek black outfits against bright cyan backdrop. Pre-chorus color shift to hot pink. Chorus
explodes with rapid cuts between wide shots of choreography and close-ups of faces and
fashion details — each cut synced to the snare."`
